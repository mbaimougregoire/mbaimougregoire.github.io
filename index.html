About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in IADI laboratory (Université de Lorraine). My Ph.D. thesis is entitled Quantitative MRI for human placental vascularization. Mainly, I work on super-resolution reconstruction, IVIM (Intravoxel Incoherent Motion) parameters estimation, image registration and ex vivo placental vascular analysis (placental vascular segmentation, arteries/vein separation). I was fellow in Fatima Fellowship , a pre-doctoral program. I was mentored by Marouen Ben Guebila (Harvad University).

Research Interests

My research interests are :

  • super-resolution reconstruction
  • Image registration
  • Quantitative MR parameters estimation (IVIM, T2-w, T2*w)
  • Image classification, image segmentation and object detection
